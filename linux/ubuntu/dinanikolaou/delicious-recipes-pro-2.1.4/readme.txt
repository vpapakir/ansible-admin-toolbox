== Description ==

Delicious Recipes Pro includes tons of extra essential features to make it easier to publish and share your recipes with bloggers. In the Pro Version, the analytics dashboard feature helps you to analyze the stats of every published, pending and submitted recipe in terms of total likes, and ratings. The Frontend Recipe Submission helps to garner increased user engagement and conversions as it allows you to customize and attach a recipe submission form on your site. To help monetize your blog, you can now promote different equipments you use using your affiliate links. In addition, a handy Floating Recipe bar provides quick access to essential features on the go such as focused reading more, timer, social sharing and much more.
